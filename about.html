Our Story

15+ Years of Undefeated Trust

Prime Facility Supplies was founded with one simple goal: to make office procurement easy, reliable and cost-effective for businesses of all sizes. Over 15 years, we have grown into Delhi NCR's most trusted one-stop office supply partner.

Today we serve 350+ registered clients including corporates, SMEs, schools, hospitals and government offices. Our 8000+ product range covers everything from everyday stationery to specialized cleaning, pantry essentials and corporate gifting.

Led by Samar, our team is committed to delivering not just products, but a complete hassle-free office supply experience: fast delivery, competitive pricing and dedicated personal support.
